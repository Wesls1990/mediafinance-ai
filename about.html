Mission

Take the strain off overworked production accountants.

We’re two industry professionals with 30+ years’ experience. Our aim is simple: remove manual entry, reduce reconciliation pain, and make approvals airtight — so finance teams can focus on production, not paperwork.

Pain points we eliminate

Inaccuracy

AI validations at input, hard-coded compliance rules, and reconciliations that flag issues before they hit cost reports.

Time wasting
